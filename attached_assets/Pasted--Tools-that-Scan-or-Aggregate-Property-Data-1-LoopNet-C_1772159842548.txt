🧰 Tools that Scan or Aggregate Property Data
1. LoopNet – Commercial Listings & Basic Data

Free marketplace for commercial buildings across the U.S.

Agents can search property listings, filter by asset type, city, price, square footage, etc.

Good for quick property scans when sourcing deals.

May require a free account.

Use case: Scan by city or property type to see availability and basic details.

2. CIMLS – Free Commercial Real Estate Database

Completely free CRE database with searchable commercial listings (sale & lease).

Filters for city, price, size, property type.

No signup required for basic browsing.

Use case: Quick broad scans for commercial inventory and comp research.

3. CompStak – Free CRE Lease/Sales Comps

Professional database used by brokers with lease and sales comparables.

Accessible “free” data/analytics for agents.

Provides deeper transaction data than simple listings.

Use case: Pull comps for valuation or negotiation support.

4. Property Address Scanner (Chrome Extension)

A free scanning tool that extracts property addresses from webpages.

Takes lists of addresses online and turns them into spreadsheets you can export.

Works with Zillow, Redfin, listings, PDF pages, market reports.

Use case: If you find a list of addresses (e.g., LoopNet results), you can automatically extract them into a CSV.

🧠 Deep Property Data Tools (Some Free, Some Paid)
5. PropertyShark – Commercial Property Details

Offers property details, ownership info, tax history, comps, and maps.

Free account gives limited basic info; paid gives full access.

Good for digging into property records and portfolios.

Use case: Research building attributes & ownership before outreach.

6. Reonomy – Commercial Data Intelligence

One of the best sources for ownership, transaction history, building details, and market insights.

Offers a free trial for agents to scan large datasets.

Includes predictive metrics and portfolio profiling.

Use case: Deep scans for off-market leads and owner contact info.

7. ATTOM Property Navigator

National property data coverage including commercial insights (values, foreclosure, AVM).

Often part of free/entry-level products — powerful scan tool for U.S. property database queries.

🧠 Insights Tools for Property Data

These aren’t “scanners” per se, but help agents aggregate or interpret property data quickly:

RPR (Realtors Property Resource) – Generates AI-powered property summaries for listed properties (residential and commercial) if you’re a Realtor.

CoreLogic & HouseCanary models – Property valuation and analytics used by agents, sometimes accessible via free reports/tools.

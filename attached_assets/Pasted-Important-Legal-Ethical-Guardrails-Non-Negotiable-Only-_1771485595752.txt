Important Legal & Ethical Guardrails (Non-Negotiable)

Only target business phone numbers (main lines, sales, office) — never personal cell phones of owners unless they are publicly listed as the business contact.
Always respect TCPA/CAN-SPAM/state laws: Business-to-business calls have fewer restrictions, but you must scrub against the National Do Not Call Registry and honor opt-outs.
Never use aggressive scraping that violates website ToS (Google, LinkedIn, Facebook are strict).
Stick to public records + APIs + voluntary published info.

The agent will use a cascading enrichment workflow (try free/public first, then paid) so it stays cheap and compliant.
